# Perfectionism

A pattern of over-checking, over-optimizing, or over-performing in order to avoid error, criticism, shame, or loss of control.

## Can appear as
- endless revision
- difficulty shipping
- over-preparing
- self-criticism

## Questions
- What am I afraid will happen if this is imperfect?
- What am I trying to prove?
- What would be enough?
