# Passage

What is a car? It is a specific combination of engine, fuel, doors, windshield - all composite materials. Those composites come from base materials. Base materials are found in nature. A material refers to the matter rather than the form.

## Context

What are we? Are we our body? Are we our mind? We are a specific combination of genetics, given physiology, social environment, upbringing.
