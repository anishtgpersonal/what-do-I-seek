# Passage

The story leads us and we follow, sometimes happy, sometimes resisting, struggling, blank, and wondering. Who would ever know how a story slips out of our hands. We are never in complete control. Like a piece of music, all our remembrances, the chance encounters, the things that happened to us and the way we responded, or did not, float in and out of memory like breath and life to make up a kaleidoscope.

## My Notes

We make sense of our lives only after we've lived them. In retrospect.
