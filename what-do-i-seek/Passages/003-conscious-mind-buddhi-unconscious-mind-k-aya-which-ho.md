# Passage

Conscious mind (buddhi); unconscious mind (kāśaya), which holds impressions, whether processed by the intellect or not. Most of our lives might be dictated by what is in this unconscious. What this consists of, when it is formed, and why it holds certain things and not others, I do not know. The unconscious holds my values. It holds my resentments, fears, disappointments and complexes.
