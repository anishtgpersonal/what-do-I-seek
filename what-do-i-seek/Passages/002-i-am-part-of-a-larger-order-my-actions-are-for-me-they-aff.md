# Passage

I am part of a larger order. My actions are for me. They affect others, so I have to do what is required at that point. The results are not with me. Some go my way, most don't. I cannot stop. A water fountain does not stop because it thinks it is worthless. It simply flows. Others may ignore it, drink from it, bathe in it or use it to put out a fire. The fountain keeps flowing either way.
